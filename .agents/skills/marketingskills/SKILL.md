# Marketing Skills: SEO & Content Optimization

This skill provides guidelines for running SEO audits, programmatic content generation, and creative optimization for Pinterest/social traffic channels.

## Strategy & Checklist

### 1. Title & Meta Optimization
- Verify title tags and descriptions are structured to maximize Click-Through Rate (CTR).
- Maintain proper heading hierarchy (one `<h1>` per page, sequential `<h2>`/`<h3>` tags).

### 2. Social Media Description Optimization
- Generate engaging, hook-driven descriptions for Pinterest posts:
  - Include relevant, highly searched keywords naturally.
  - End with a clear call-to-action (CTA).
  - Use structured hashtags relevant to the content theme.

### 3. Campaign Performance Tracking
- Check metrics periodically:
  - Identify CTR% trends.
  - Audit impressions and click conversions.
  - If CTR falls below the target baseline, trigger creative calibration.
